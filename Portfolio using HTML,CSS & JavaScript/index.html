My Services

Bronze Casting

Bronze casting is a traditional sculptural technique involving the pouring of molten bronze into a mold to create intricate and durable metal sculptures.

Modelling

Modelling in sculptures involves shaping and manipulating materials such as clay, wax, or plaster to create three-dimensional forms and figures.

Decorative Sculptures

Decorative sculptures are artistic creations designed to enhance aesthetic appeal in various settings, often featuring intricate designs, motifs, and themes.

Clay Work

Clay work in sculptures involves shaping and molding pliable clay into desired forms, allowing for detailed and expressive creations before firing or casting.

Religious Sculpture

Religious sculpture: Artistic representation of deities and spiritual narratives, serving as objects of worship and cultural significance.
